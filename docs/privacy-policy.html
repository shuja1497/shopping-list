Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Grocery List ("the App"), developed by Zeno Labs, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

In short: Grocery List does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. The App:

  • Does not require an account or registration
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other device sensors

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App (shopping list items, categories, and preferences) is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is encrypted using SQLCipher for your security

Internet Access

The App does not require or use an internet connection. It functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. There are no advertisements in the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
